🍹 About SqueezeIt

SqueezeIt is more than just a drink — it's a burst of bold, fruity goodness in every drop. Made from real fruit juice and natural ingredients, SqueezeIt delivers a refreshing twist on hydration. Whether you're lounging in the sun, heading to class, or powering through a gaming session, SqueezeIt brings the flavor that fuels your day — naturally.

No artificial colors. No fake stuff. Just real juice, real flavor, and real fun.

🌈 Our Flavours

  • 🍋 Lemon Burst – A sharp citrusy zap of pure lemon energy. Tangy, zesty, and unapologetically bold — the classic rebooted.
  • 🍊 Orange Rush – Sunny, sweet, and smooth. Packed with juicy orange flavor, this one's like sipping summer.
  • 🍓 Strawberry Sizzle – Sweet meets sassy. Real strawberry vibes with a little sparkle that makes your taste buds dance.
  • 🍑 Peach Pop – Soft, juicy peach meets a light fizz. Delicate and smooth, this one’s a chill favorite.
  • 🥥 Coconut Chill – Creamy and cool like an island breeze. Pure coconut satisfaction with a mellow finish.
  • 🥝 Kiwi Zing – Tart, sweet, and slightly electric. Kiwi Zing brings the green punch you didn’t know you needed.
